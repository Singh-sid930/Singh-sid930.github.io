# Uljhanein

kuch uljhan me daal diatha un sawalon ne

Shayad jawab zehen me na aaye

ya fir alfaz labon tak aakar theher gaye

ye kabhi hum samajh na paae

Us sannate me nazrein hi bol detin kuch agar

Shayad uljhanein kuch jaati sulajh

koshish to dil ki thi bohot magar

nazron ko hi shayad na thi samajh
