Flapping flight analysis of birds

Part of senior design project. Involved the analysis of the wings of birds during the various phases of flapping flight to analyze the regions of high vibration using rolling shutter effect of camers. Link

Autonomous Vehicle Development for Mahindra Rise Challenge

Safe planning and control of multi-rotor drone fleets performing complex missions has been a challenging problem. Methods that offer guarantees on safety and mission satisfaction generally do not scale well. On the other hand, more computationally tractable approaches do not offer any safety guarantees. This project develops methods that overcomes these limitations for a wide variety of missions, e.g. the video below shows a mission where 2 pairs of drones are tasked with patrolling two regions within predefined time intervals, and while avoiding a no-fly zone and collisions with each other.

Single-seater all terrain vehicle design for SAE Baja competitions

A national racing event of all terrain vehicles for designing and demonstrating capabilities of all terrain vehicles. This project is a odd one out in the list projects here solely because I have always found this very cool and still gives me a rush.

Real-time Semantic mapping and prediction

The project involves creation of a perception pipeline capable of performing real time detection of objects, creating a semantic occupancy map and performing predictions based on the map. Link
